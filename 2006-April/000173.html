jlm wrote:
> I am trying to use control keys to navigate the sidebar. I want control-j to move down an entry, control-k to move up an entry, and
> control-o to open the mailbox. I have these keybindings in my .muttngrc file:
> 
> bind index ^k sidebar-prev
> bind index ^j sidebar-next
> bind index ^o sidebar-open
> 
> When I type one of these key combinations muttng tells me that the key is not bound. When I look at the help it looks like it is bound.
> What am I doing wrong here?

Modify your .muttngrc to look like this:

	bind index \Ck sidebar-prev
	bind index \Cj sidebar-next
	bind index \Co sidebar-open

Hope this helps,
