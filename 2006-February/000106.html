> * Trey Sizemore <trey at fastmail.fm> [02-27-06 09:19]:
> > I have another IMAP account that I'd like to read with mutt-ng.  But
> > from what I've been able to research, it appears that setting up
> > multiple accounts is a bit tricky.
> 
> 
> mailboxes 'imap://<mail-name>@<provider-address>'
> mailboxes 'imap://<mail-name2>@<provider-address2>'
> account-hook imap://<mail-name>@<provider-address> 'set imap_user=<mail-name> imap_pass=<acct-passwd>'
> account-hook imap://<mail-name2>@<provider-address2> 'set imap_user=<mail-name2> imap_pass=<acct2-passwd>'
> 
> 
> as:
> mailboxes 'imap://fred@ny.rr.com'
> mailboxes 'impa://fred@nospam.net'
> account-hook imap://fred@ny.rr.com 'set imap_user=fred imap_pass=passwd'
> account-hook imap://fred@nospam 'set imap_user=fred imap_pass=passwd'
